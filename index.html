How it works

A check-up, a plan, and a friend who checks in

Ailly isn't a video library or a firehose of tips. It's a guide that learns where you are and walks with you.

Take the 2-minute check-up

A few friendly questions about your life or work and how you use AI today. There are no wrong answers — honest is what works.

Get your personal game plan

Ailly grades where you stand, shows your biggest opportunities, and lays out exactly what to do first — in plain words.

Practice a little every day

A short daily lesson, a real-life challenge, and a Prompt Coach drill. Small wins that add up faster than you'd think.

Check in weekly — and watch your plan evolve

Each week Ailly asks how it's going and reshapes your journey around what's working. Your score climbs; so does your confidence.

Why Ailly exists

A note from the founder

"AI is the biggest shift in how people live and work since the internet — and the people it could help the most feel locked out of it. The jargon, the hype, the quiet fear of looking silly for asking. Ailly is my answer: an ally, not a course. It meets you exactly where you are, gives you one small win at a time, and never makes you feel behind. If it ever falls short of feeling like it's on your side, I want to know — the feedback buttons in the app really do land on my desk."

— Amaan · Founder, Ailly
