How it works

A check-up, a plan, and a guide who checks in

Ailly isn't a video library or a firehose of tips. It's a guide that learns where you are and walks with you.

A few friendly questions about your life or work and how you use AI today. There are no wrong answers — honest is what works.

Get your personal game plan

Ailly grades where you stand, shows your biggest opportunities, and lays out exactly what to do first — in plain words.
