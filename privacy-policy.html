Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Information we collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. During the AI diagnostic, we collect the answers you provide to personalize your AI recommendations.

How we use your information

Your answers are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate personalized recommendations. We store your profile and generated plans in Firebase so you can access them later. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Third-party services

Ailly uses Firebase (by Google) for authentication and data storage, and Anthropic's Claude API for AI-generated recommendations. You may also sign in via Apple or Google. Each service processes data according to their own privacy policies.

Enterprise interview data

When your company runs an Ailly Enterprise program, your interview answers are stored encrypted and scoped to your company. Leadership sees readiness levels, tiers, and opportunities — never your word-for-word personal answers. Company data is never used to train models or shared with other customers, and is fully deleted on request.

Data retention

Your account data and saved plans are kept until you delete your account. You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Account section in Settings.

Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS. Your account is protected by Firebase Authentication. Any API keys you enter are stored in your device's secure Keychain, never in plain text.

Your rights

You may access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time using the in-app Settings. For additional requests, contact us at sattar.amaan@gmail.com.

Children's privacy

Ailly is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

Questions? Email us at sattar.amaan@gmail.com.
